EW BROWN FIELD CHECKLIST
========================


ARRIVAL INSPECTION
------------------

Equipment cabinet:

  [ ] Power strip       — orange light ON
  [ ] RUT241 router     — 3 small green lights blinking
  [ ] Netgear switch    — green power light ON
                          green lights on three grey ethernet ports
                          orange light on blue LAN port
  [ ] Pi                — green AND orange lights on ethernet port


HARD DRIVE SWAP PROCEDURE
--------------------------

*** CRITICAL TO FOLLOW THIS ORDER PRECISELY ***

  1. Unplug power to Pi
  2. Swap SSD hard drive (leave cable in place)
  3. Confirm new drive is fully seated
  4. Plug power back into Pi
  5. Wait 60 seconds for Pi to fully boot
  6. Write today's date and your initials on the removed drive
  7. Write today's date and initials on log in enclosure

DEPARTURE CONFIRMATION
-----------------------

  *** CRITICAL ***

  [ ] 1. PI POWER IS PLUGGED BACK IN
  [ ] 2. PI ORANGE AND GREEN ETHERNET LIGHTS ARE ON
  [ ] 3. Removed drive is labeled with date and initials
  [ ] 4. Close lid to enclosure


CAMERA CHECKS
--------------

  [ ] 1. Check cables for wear or rodent damage
  [ ] 2. Check tripods and camera orientation
  [ ] 3. Wipe lenses carefully with provided lens wipes
